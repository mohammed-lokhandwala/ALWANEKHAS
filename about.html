We Are Alwaanekhass

Welcome to Alwaanekhass, where the love for food meets the art of culinary craftsmanship. Founded by the dynamic trio of a visionary father and his two culinary prodigies, Mohammed Ajmeer, and Burhan Ajmer, our venture is a celebration of flavors, traditions, and the joy of sharing a good meal.

Our primary focus is on deliveries, ensuring that the exquisite taste of Alwaanekhass reaches your doorstep. Whether you're craving a comforting bowl of biryani, a flavorful curry, or a sweet indulgence, our diverse menu is designed to cater to every palate. We take pride in sourcing the finest ingredients, employing time-honored recipes, and infusing each dish with the warmth of home-cooked goodness.
